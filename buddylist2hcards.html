iChat buddy list to hCards

AppleScript

Download

Click buddylist2hcards.scpt and save to your Mac. Open and run the script to convert your entire buddy list to a valid XHTML 1.0 Strict list of clickable hCards.

Requirements

  • MacOSX
  • iChat
  • BBEdit
